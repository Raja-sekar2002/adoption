Doggy Daycare

Exercise and socialization are important for dogs, and we provide plenty of both at doggy daycare. This service offers a safe, fun, and nurturing space for your dog to romp and play in the fresh air while you’re away for the day. They’ll get plenty of interaction with other dogs as well as get the attention of dog-loving humans. Best of all? You’ll be able to enjoy a blissfully pooped-out pup by the end of the day.
When considering daycare for your puppy, it's essential to choose a reputable facility with experienced and caring staff. Our puppy daycare in Newburgh can help. Introducing your puppy to daycare gradually and observing their reactions can also help ensure a positive experience for your furry friend.

Engaging Group Play for Social Dogs

Group play gives your well-socialized dog the opportunity to visit with other friendly, outgoing dogs who, like your pet, have all been vaccinated, neutered, and temperament tested. Our certified staff trained in group dynamics and canine body language provide a safe way for your pet to interact and play well with others with constant and consistent direction and correction, when necessary. Your dog can play with other dogs and then when he’s tired, he can take a nap next to his new best friend.

Specialized Puppy Daycare Program

Watching adorable puppies play together is enough to warm the heart of any human bystander. At puppy daycare, your puppy between the ages of 8 weeks to 8 months old will learn how to socialize properly and grow into a friendly, well-mannered adult dog. Your puppy will not only have fun but gain confidence while working on their canine communication in a safe, upbeat atmosphere with lots of new friends.

Top Benefits of Puppy Daycare in Newburgh

Daycare for puppies can offer several benefits for both the puppy and the owner. Here are some of the advantages of sending your puppy to daycare:

  • Socialization
  • Exercise and Physical Activity
  • Mental Stimulation
  • Prevention of Separation Anxiety
  • Housebreaking and Training Assistance
  • Confidence Building
  • Convenience for Owners
  • Monitoring of Health and Behavior
